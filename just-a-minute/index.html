In a Minute (JAM)

The topic for the JAM will be provided 10 minutes prior to the event. The event will be held through Google meet. Participants will have to talk for a minute based on the given topic. The talk will be evaluated based on the presentation and fluency. Avoid repetition and deviation from the topic.

Description

"If you can speak, you can influence. If you can influence, you can change lives."
Are you looking for an opportunity to show off your oratory skills? Do you think you can express your views and opinions in a minute without hesitation, repetition and deviation?
IEEE Encamp presents In a Minute, JAM session for all the students. Inviting all students to showcase their impromptu speaking skills.

▪︎Topics will be given to the participants 10 minutes prior to the event.
