Just A Minute (JAM)

Name of the event is In a Minute. Topic will be provided within FIVE minutes prior to the event. The program will be held through Google Meet. The main moto for the event is to talk for one minute on the topic provided. The presentation will be evaluated by presentation skills and fluency of the student. It is important to avoid repeatation and deviation from the topic.
